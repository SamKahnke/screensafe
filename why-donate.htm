Why Donote

The Screen Safe program is currently in production, but donations are needed to bring this resource to completion.  As of October 2018, Rivertown Communications has donated $21,000 in design and production labor.  Please considering partnering with our team by making a donation. Your gift of $100. $50 or less will make a difference to a multitude of children and families struggling with compulsive social media use. With this issue, education is the most effective answer.

Donate today to help us meet our timeline.

Phase 1, the Screen Safe e-learning program, is scheduled for release in the 4th quarter of 2018. Phase 2 (the Discussion Guide) and Phase 3 (the Youth Version with Certificate) will follow quickly in the 1st quarter of 2019. Funding is needed for all 3 phases.

When complete, the e-learning courses and study guide will be available to the general public at a nominal cost, and the funds generated  will be used to provide ongoing support and updates to the Screen Safe program. Because this technology changes daily, frequent updates are required. Donations in excess of the amount needed will be used to help the homeless in St. Paul, MN, through Frontier Fellowship (501c3), a non-denominational, Christian social ministry. Receipts will be issued for all donations.

All donors will be notified when the program is available for use. Anyone contributing over $25 will also receive token(s) to access the Screen Safe programs.
